Privacy Policy

Effective June 10, 2026  ·  App version 1.0 and later

The short version

Pocket Felt does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

What is stored on your device

Local data only

Pocket Felt stores the following data locally on your device using iOS's standard UserDefaults system. None of it leaves your device.

  • Your saved (starred) games
  • Your score pads — player names, round scores, and game settings
  • Whether you have unlocked the Dice Roller in-app purchase
  • App preferences

What happens when you delete the app

All locally stored data is permanently removed from your device when you delete Pocket Felt. There is no server-side backup. iCloud sync is not currently supported.

What we do not collect

Pocket Felt collects no data of any kind about you or your device. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No third-party SDKs, trackers, or analytics frameworks are included in the app

The app's declared data practice with Apple is "Data Not Collected."

In-App Purchases

Handled entirely by Apple

The Dice Roller unlock and optional tips are processed by Apple's App Store payment system. Pocket Felt never sees your payment details, Apple ID, or any purchase-related personal information. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

The app records only a boolean flag locally — "dice roller purchased: yes/no" — to avoid making a network call every time you open the app.

Internet access

Pocket Felt makes no network requests of its own. The app works entirely offline. The only exception is the standard App Store purchase flow when you tap "Unlock" or "Restore Purchases" — those requests go directly to Apple's servers and are not routed through us.

Children

Pocket Felt is rated 4+ on the App Store. Because the app collects no data, it is safe for users of any age under applicable laws including COPPA and GDPR-K.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what data the app collects or how it is used, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. We will not change our data practices without updating this policy first.
